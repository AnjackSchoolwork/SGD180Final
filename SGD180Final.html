Deep Delver

Instructions below

You control Bob the block.
Bob is a simple block who just happens to possess the amazing ability to shoot balls of plasma out of his face.
Somehow, Bob has gotten himself trapped in this strange dungeon. The only way out is through a locked door.
Please help Bob find the key at the bottom of the dungeon and then climb back up to escape through the door.

Controls:

A - Move Bob to the left
D - Move Bob to the right
Spacebar - Make Bob jump

Left Mouse - Make Bob fire a ball of burning plasma
